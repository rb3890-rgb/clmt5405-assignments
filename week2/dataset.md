# Dataset — Mauna Loa Atmospheric CO2 (Monthly Mean)

**What it is.** Monthly mean atmospheric carbon dioxide (CO2) dry-air mole fraction in parts per million (ppm), measured at Mauna Loa Observatory, Hawaii, from March 1958 to the present.

**Where it lives.**
- Source page: https://gml.noaa.gov/ccgg/trends/data.html
- Direct file: https://gml.noaa.gov/webdata/ccgg/trends/co2/co2_mm_mlo.csv
- DOI: https://doi.org/10.15138/yaf1-bk21

**Format.** CSV (plain text; comment lines start with `#`, followed by numeric columns: year, month, decimal date, average, deseasonalized, ndays, sdev, unc).

**License.** Public domain. Produced by NOAA, a U.S. government agency, and not subject to copyright protection in the United States; NOAA requests citation as good practice.

**Provenance.** Created by the NOAA Global Monitoring Laboratory (GML), Boulder, Colorado. Current data contacts: Dr. Xin Lan (NOAA/GML) and Dr. Ralph Keeling (Scripps Institution of Oceanography). The 1958-1974 portion originates from C. David Keeling (Scripps).
